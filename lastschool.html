Yangchenphug Highger Secondary School

The school was started in 1965 by His Majesty Jigme Dorji Wangchuck. Initially the school started as a co-ed, residential school named Thimphu Public School, run on the pattern of Public schools in India. The school had classes from Kindergarten to four. The first faculty included 6 teachers.

Launched On: 1965

1976:Thimphu Public School was renamed to Yangchenphug Central School

Location:Thimphu
