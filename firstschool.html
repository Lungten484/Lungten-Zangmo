Thrimshing Middle Secondary School

Thrimshing Central School (TCS) was officially consecrated and inaugurated by HRH The Gyaltshab Jigme Dorji Wangchuck on September 16. The construction of the school was completed on September 3 last year at a total cost of Nu 141.43 million, which was funded by the Government of India.

Launched On: April-2015

Location:Tashigang, Thrimshing
